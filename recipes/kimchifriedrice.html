Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

When it comes to Korean comfort foods, there is nothing like a classic yet simple dish such as kimchi fried rice. This dish is quick and easy to make; beloved by not only Koreans but anyone who loves fried rice and can handle a bit of heat.

If you go to any Korean bunsikjip, a Korean restaurant that specializes in casual and convenient meals, chances are, you will find kimchi fried rice on their menu. But instead of having to go out of your way to eat this dish, you can make this meal in the comfort of your home!

Ingredients

  • 3 bowls steamed rice (3 cups)
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi (should be aged, well-fermented and sour)
  • ¼ cup kimchi juice (spicy red juice from a jar of kimchi)
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoons gochujang
  • 3 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon neutral oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 sheet of gim, roasted and shredded into seaweed strips

Directions

  1. Heat up a wide, flat pan or skillet. Add the vegetable oil.
  2. Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute over medium-high heat.
  3. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Stir all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes with a wooden spoon.
  4. Add sesame oil and remove from the heat.
  5. Garnish with sprinkled chopped green onion, roasted gim, and sesame seeds. Serve right away.
